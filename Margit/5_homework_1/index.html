Age of the user

Make a program that asks the age of the user. If the age is less than 18 the text "too young" is printed. If tha age is less than 27 the text "Right age for military service" is printed. If the age is less than 41 the text "You are in reserve" is printed. If the age is less than 55 the text "You are in backup service" is printed. All other cases "too aged" is printed.
